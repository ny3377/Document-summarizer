About Document Summarizer

Document summarization is a process that condenses the content of a document while retaining its essential information. It aims to provide a concise representation of the main points within the text, making it easier for users to comprehend large volumes of information quickly. In the provided code, the document summarization method involves tokenizing the text into words and sentences, calculating the frequency of each word, scoring sentences based on word frequency, determining an average score, and generating a summary by selecting sentences with scores above a certain threshold. Finally, the summarized content is displayed on the webpage for user convenience.
